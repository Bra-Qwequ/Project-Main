How to Prepare Pasta

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water.
  2. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions.
  3. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  4. Drain the cooked pasta using a colander.
  5. Prepare your favorite sauce while the pasta cooks.
  6. Mix the drained pasta with the sauce.
  7. Add cheese, herbs, or other toppings if desired.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
